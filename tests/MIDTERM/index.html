Mission Statement

Connecting Through Charity provides year-round giving to the local community to help children, families, and individuals in need overcome hardship.

Vision Statement

Staying connected in the local community to positively impact the life of others.

Projects

Some of the projects we do: Make Blessing Bags for the homeless, provide Christmas gifts to families in need within our local school districts, providing Easter Baskets to families for their children, we adopted a teacher and her classroom this year for the first time, we have provided the food for families to make a Thanksgiving dinner.
We are continuously adding new projects.
