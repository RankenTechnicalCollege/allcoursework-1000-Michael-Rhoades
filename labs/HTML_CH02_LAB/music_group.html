Best albums

Ocerina of Time
The soundtrack to Ocerina of time features some of the most iconic pieces of music in zelda, and the themes are often remixed in other games. these include Zelda's Lullaby, Saria's Song, and Gerudo Valley.
Breath of the Wild
The soundtrack to Breath of the Wild is more subtle than a lot of the other game's soundtracks, but it works really well because it is meant to be much calmer and draw you in more. Some of my favorite songs are Kass's Theme, the Champion's Themes(Mipha, Daruk, Revali, and Urbosa), and Korok Forest.
A Link Between Worlds
Part of this choice is because I just finished the game, but it remasters a lot of the soundtracks used in other 2d Zelda games that bring a sense of adventure. Some of my favorites are Ravio's theme, Hyrule Field, and Kakariko Village.
Honorable Mentions
I wanted to include a few songs from games that didn't quite make the list, like Fi's Farewell from Skyward Sword, Mipha's Lament from Twilight Princess, or the trailer theme for Tears of the Kingdom
