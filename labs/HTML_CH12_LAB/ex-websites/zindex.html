When the three golden goddesses Din, Nayru, and Farore left this world, they left behind the Triforce. The golden Triangles allowed anyone, If they have a balanced intent between Power, Wisdom, and Courage, to have any wish they desire. If they have an unbalanced intent, the Triforce splits into 3 pieces, one staying with the original user and the other 2 go to people embodying the other traits.
