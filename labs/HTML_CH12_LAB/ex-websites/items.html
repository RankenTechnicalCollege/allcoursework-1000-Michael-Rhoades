Link uses many items throughout the series. Here are some of the notable ones.

Hookshot

Chain hook that sticks to wood

Clawshot

Attaches to grates and vines. May come as a pair

Boomerang

Flies a short distance before returning

Bombs

They explode

Power Bracelet

Allows you to lift heavy items

Red Potion

Heals Link

Green Potion

Refills Magic

Blue Potion

Refills both Health and Magic

Fairy

Revives Link if he dies

Bow

Allows Link to shoot arrows

Bombchu

Timer bomb that runs for a bit before exploding

Flute

Fast travel in early games

Magic Powder

Does weird stuff on things it went on

Iron Boots

Makes you heavy

Hammer

Pushes down stakes, damages enemies
