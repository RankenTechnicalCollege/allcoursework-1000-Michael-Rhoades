Over the millennia that the Zelda series takes place, there are many races and groups that appear.

Major races

Hylians

Also the term for people from Hyrule, Hylians have pointed, elf like ears that allow them to hear the gods. This ability is rare, so they are pretty much humans.

Kokiri

Living by the Great Deku Tree and the Lost woods, these forest children never age beyond a child. They all have fairy companions and link grew up with them one time.

Sheikah

This is a sub-race of Hylians that have white hair. They live in the shadows serving the royal family of Hyrule, leading to a very bloody history.

Gorons

These rock-like and rock eating people live on and and around the Death Mountain volcano. They are always happy to assist a brother in need.

Sea Zora

These Fish-people live in Lanayru. They have impressive architecture and are generally peaceful.

River Zora

These Fish-people live in Lake Hylia and other rivers and lakes. They are much more hostile to non-Zora.

Rito

These Bird people come from the Zora live in the mountains. They are generally solitary, but are friendly with visiters to their town.

Gerudo

This group of warrior women only have one male born every 100 years. The male will normally become their king, but after Ganondorf, they seem to no longer accept a king.

Koroks

These plant people come from the Kokiri, and also live in the lost woods. Other than being more plant'like, they are very similar to the Kokiri.

Minor races

Anouki

Snow people. Opposed to the Yook.

Ancient Robots

These robots are mostly all destroyed by the time you meet them.

Deku Scrub

Plant creatures that typically attack on sight.

Lokomo

train/hylian hybrids. Similar to Sheikah.

Minish

Small, mouse-like people. Capable of magic.

Mogma

Mole-like people. They dig lots of holes.

Oocca

Human/chicken hybrid. Helped create Hyrule.

Parella

Seahorse-like people. Similar to the Zora.

Twili

People from the realm of Twilight.

Interlopers

Twili who tried to take over Hyrule.

Zonai

Helped create the kingdom of Hyrule.

Yiga

Worshippers of Ganon, opposed to the Sheikah. Love Bananas.
