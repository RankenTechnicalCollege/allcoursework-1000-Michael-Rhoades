Board Games

I enjoy playing board games. Some of my favorites are Isle of Cats and Unmatched. Isle of cats is a strategy game where you try to pack as many cats on your board as possible. Unmatched is a game where you play as a character from literature, movies, games, history, or legends in a fight with another character. some of the characters include King Arthur, Nikola Tesla, Alice(from Alice in Wonderland), Hamlet, and the Velociraptors from Jurassic Park.
