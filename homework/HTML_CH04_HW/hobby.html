Unmatched

Unmatched is a board game that I play where you and the person/people you are playing with play as a character from literature, film, shows, games, history, legends, etc. Each character, refered to as a Hero, has their own unique deck, typically 30 cards + a character card. Each set has 1-2 boards, but it is highly encouraged to mix and match everything. The actual gameplay revolves around defeating your opponents. Most of the Heroes also have other fighters who assist them. These are called Sidekicks, and they can have their own health or only have 1 health, but have multiple of them, typically 3-4. All fighters also have a Character Ability, printed on their Character card. This is typically the main gimmick of the deck, some examples being switching between incresed attack or defence, placing tokens on the board to damage opponents, or a revive mechanic.
